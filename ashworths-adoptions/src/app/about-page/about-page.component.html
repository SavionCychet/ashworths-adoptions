WHO ARE WE?

Ashworth's Adoptions is a non-profit dog shelter located in St. Louis, MO. Our team is made up of volunteers and animal specialists who work to bring in dogs from the streets and give them new homes. Our organization was etablished this year in 2020 and so far, we've already rescued over 100 dogs!

OUR MISSION

With the rising rate of euthenization and animal abuse, we at Ashworth's devote our resources to finding abandoned or injured dogs left on the streets. Our mission is to bring them in, treat them if needed, and find new, loving homes for them. While our organization is geared towards dogs, we would one day like to have the reach and resources to rescue other animals as well, and beyond the borders of just St. Louis.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Any donations of any amount are greatly appreciated. 100% of donations go directly towards our resources for helping dogs. You can also become a temporary member of our team and become a volunteer! We currently have 34 volunteers of all ages on our team and we're always looking for more! The more hands we have helping our cause, the more dogs we can save from the streets!
